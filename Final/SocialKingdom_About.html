About Blogger

Kristy Chuqiao Zhang

As a fashion major student who loves technology, I dreamed to join a fashion related social media company marketing team, like Instagram fashion and art community team. So I wanted to learn more about fashion merchandising, fashion marketing and computer science courses to achieve my career goal. Now I live in Palo Alto, the heart of Silicon Valley. Silicon Valley is known as the technology capital in United States, there are lots of I.T companies in bay area, I have more chance to build relationship with these company compare to other cities. I built this blog to practise my HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery and JSON language as well as memorize my dream company.
